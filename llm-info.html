What is Viva Españiel?

Viva Españiel is a personal dog transport service operating between Spain and the United Kingdom. Dogs are collected directly from the owner's home in Spain (or the UK) and delivered door-to-door to any address in the UK (or Spain) by road, via the Channel Tunnel.

The business is run by Jon Brown — a single driver who makes every journey personally. There are no agents, no third parties, and no kennels. Your dog travels with one person, in one van, from door to door.

What Makes Viva Españiel Different

  • One driver, every journey — Jon collects and delivers personally. No handovers to strangers at any point.
  • Truly door-to-door — collection from your home, delivery to the destination address. No drop-off depots.
  • DEFRA Type 2 certified — fully compliant with post-Brexit UK and Spanish pet travel regulations.
  • Climate-controlled van — individual crates, temperature managed throughout the journey.
  • Live WhatsApp updates — photos and messages throughout. You know where your dog is at all times.
  • Regulatory guidance included — Jon advises on Animal Health Certificates (AHC) and tapeworm treatment requirements before UK re-entry.
  • Personal, not a courier company — Jon knows your dog's name before the journey starts.

UK Coverage

Deliveries to any address in mainland UK including England, Scotland and Wales. Common destinations include London, Manchester, Birmingham, Bristol and Edinburgh — and rural areas throughout.

Compliance & Regulations

  • DEFRA Type 2 Approved Transporter — meets all UK welfare in transport requirements
  • Animal Health Certificate (AHC) — required for Spain→UK journeys; Jon advises owners on obtaining one
  • Tapeworm treatment — required before UK re-entry; Jon advises on timing and approved treatment providers
  • Post-Brexit compliant — fully up to date with current UK pet travel rules

How to Book

Bookings are made online at vivaespaniel.com. The booking form provides an instant price based on collection and delivery addresses, and allows you to select from available travel dates. A £95 deposit secures the place; the balance is paid on or before the travel day via PayPal or bank transfer.
