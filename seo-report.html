Every competitor targets expats going TO Spain. The return journey — people moving back home, snowbirds, or those after Brexit changes — is massively underserved. Blog is now live — this should be the first post.

Transfur dominates informational content but it's air-focused. A road-transport AHC walkthrough and tapeworm treatment explainer fills a real gap. Blog infrastructure is ready — publish these first.

★★★ QUICK WIN — blog is live, write it

"Transport Labrador Spain UK", "small dog transport Spain", "Spaniel transport Spain" — all uncontested. Competitors use generic crate language; nobody targets by breed.

Every competitor hides pricing behind a contact form. A page with real price ranges ranks for "pet transport Spain cost" and builds trust — customers hate chasing quotes.
